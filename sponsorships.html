Why Sponsor Us?

Skyridge Junior Devo is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Your sponsorship is tax-deductible.

We serve 7th and 8th grade students from across the Lehi area, many of whom would not have access to competitive mountain biking without this program. Your support makes it possible.

What Your Sponsorship Funds

  • NICA registration and race fees
  • Certified coach training and background checks
  • Trail maintenance and team equipment
  • Financial assistance for riders who can't cover program costs

Why Sponsor Us?

Skyridge Junior Devo is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit — your sponsorship is tax-deductible. Make checks payable to North Utah County Cycling Club Inc. Contact Brandon Smith at 801-615-1864 for mailing address.

We serve 7th and 8th grade students from across the Lehi area. Our team is entirely volunteer-coached, so every dollar goes directly toward the team.

What Your Sponsorship Funds

  • NICA league registration and race fees
  • Team equipment and supplies
  • Certified coach training and background checks
  • Extra activities and experiences throughout the season
  • Financial assistance for riders who need it
Have a rider on the team? We'll discount your team fees $50 for every $250 donated.

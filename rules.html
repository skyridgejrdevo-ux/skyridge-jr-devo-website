Our Motto

Strong

Brave

We ride trails that scare us. We try new skills. We race even when we're nervous. Courage is a muscle.

Kind

We cheer for teammates and competitors alike. We help riders who are struggling. We treat trails, landowners, and volunteers with respect.

Key Rules Highlights

These are highlights — read the full rules document for complete expectations.

⛑️ Helmet Policy

A properly fitted, CPSC-certified helmet must be worn at all times while riding during team activities. No exceptions. MIPS helmets are strongly recommended.

🤝 Sportsmanship

Taunting, disrespectful behavior toward competitors, coaches, or race officials may result in disqualification or suspension from team activities.

🌲 Trail Etiquette

Yield to hikers and equestrians. Announce yourself before passing. Respect trail closures — no exceptions. Leave no trace at every ride location.

📱 Communication

If your rider can't attend a practice or race, notify your coach through Spond in advance. Repeated no-shows without communication may affect ride eligibility.

📸 Social Media

Riders represent Skyridge Junior Devo online. Conduct on social media is held to the same standard as in-person behavior.

🎓 Academics

Per NICA rules, riders must maintain a 2.0 GPA or higher to be eligible to participate and race. Academic eligibility is checked before each race season.

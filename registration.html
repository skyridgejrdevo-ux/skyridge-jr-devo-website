Register for the 2026 Season

Registration is a two-step process. Both steps must be completed before your rider can participate in practices or races.

1

Team Registration

Register directly with Skyridge Junior Devo using the form below.

Team fee: TBD — a coach will confirm at kickoff

  • Fill out the registration form below
  • A coach will follow up with payment instructions within 48 hours
Financial assistance is available — check the box on the form or contact a coach.
2

NICA Registration

Register your rider with the Utah High School Cycling League (UHSCL) through NICA PitZone.

NICA fee: $330 — Jr. Devo league fee ($270) + NICA Athlete base ($55) + tech fee ($5). Late fee of +$25 applies after June 1. Last day to register with NICA: June 5. Verify at utahmtb.org/registration.

  • Returning riders: Once you've completed Step 1, log in directly at pitzone.nationalmtb.org to renew your NICA registration.
  • New riders: Once you submit the team registration form in Step 1, we'll create your rider's PitZone account. You'll receive an email from NICA PitZone in order to complete your rider's NICA registration.
NICA registration must be complete before your rider can race.

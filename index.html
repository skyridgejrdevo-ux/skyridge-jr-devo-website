Why Join Devo?

More than racing — it's a community

🚵

Build Fitness & Skills

Train on real trails with certified ride leaders who focus on fundamentals — cornering, climbing, technical descents — in a structured, safe environment.

🏁

Race & Explore

Devo races are about fun, adventure, and personal growth — not standings. Riders compete in beginner, intermediate, or advanced categories on real mountain bike courses. Show up, push yourself, and enjoy the ride.

🤝

Be Part of Something

From team breakfasts to race-day cheering to post-season celebrations, Devo is a community. Riders build friendships and confidence that go far beyond mountain biking.
